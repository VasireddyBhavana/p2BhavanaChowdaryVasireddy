Car DashBoard

C. 5 Examples of Car Dashboards

1.Ford Escape 2017



Pros:

  1. The Dashboard has all the Information that the user requires.
  2. In the dashboard the speedometer and tachometer are in the larger size making it easier for the user to notice.
  3. Fuel and Engine Temperature Guages are also clearly diaplayed.

Cons:

  1. We can find the Warning symbols both in the tachometer and speedometer and few of them are turned on, this may confuse the use to find if there is any issue or not.

2.Audi Q3 2014



Pros:

  1. In this dashboard space utilization is efficient.
  2. The Dashboard has all the Information that the user requires.
  3. In the dashboard the speedometer and tachometer are in the larger size making it easier for the user to notice.
  4. Multiple options on the screen.

Cons:

  1. Fuel and Engine Temperature Guages are not clear as user couldn't find it clearly with a glance.
  2. Both the speedometer and tachometer designs are similar which is hard to differentiate in a glance.

3.Ford Edge 2007



Pros:

  1. The Dashboard has all the main guages like speedometer, Tachometer, fuel guage, enginee temp guage.
  2. All the Emergency symbols are visible clearly.

Cons:

  1. The Speedometer and Tachometer are identical to one another and also they seems to be like a weighing scale.

4.Kia Rio 2017



Pros:

  1. Visibility of speedometer is good.
  2. Visibility of fuel and engine temp guages are also goood.

Cons:

  1. The digital display is kind of small and has poor visibility.
  2. Essential warning alerts are missing.

5.Chevorlet Traverse 2017



Pros:

  1. The size of speedometer and tachometer is good.
  2. Fuel and temperature guage bars are clear.

Cons:

  1. The Dashboard seems to be little conjusted.
  2. Even though all the warning alerts are present they are located in a small amount of space.

B. Car Dashboard Uage and Proposed Design

Common Car Dashboard Uses

  1. Tracking the speed.
  2. Tracking the RPM's.
  3. Checks for fuel guage.
  4. Checks for Engine Temperature.
  5. Checks for Engine Oil.
  6. Turn Signals are also most commonly used.
  7. Safety notifications like door lock & seat belt.
  8. Checks for Mileage.

Rare Car Dashboard Uses

  1. Checking for current car temperatures.
  2. Few Warning symbols.

Controls that User Interacts with the car dashboard

  1. Speedometer.
  2. Wipers.
  3. Indicator.
  4. Light Beams.
  5. Cruise Control.

Common Mistakes that user can make with current designs

  1. Speedometer and tachometer placement may sometimes confuse the user as mostly they are designed with similar outlook.
  2. Considering warning alerts as an emergency issue.
  3. Addition of too many controls to the dashboard may make it look more clumsy and can create confusion to the user.
  4. Proper utilization of space.

Suggested Improvements to Design

  1. Properly distinguishing the Tachometer and Speedometer.
  2. Digital display for required controls like to show current speed etc.
  3. Placing only the most essential controls on the dashboard.
  4. Proper utilization of Space and placing the controls accordingly.
